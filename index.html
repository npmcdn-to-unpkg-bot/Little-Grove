Little Grove


Little Grove Community Interest Company is a registered not-for-profit social enterprise based in Bushey, Hertfordshire. We work with children and young people who have learning disabilities & provide them with support services during the day, in the evenings and during weekends & school holidays.
We also offer regular overnight stays and are open all year round. At Little Grove, we provide a comfortable, home-from-home environment in which children and young people can spend time with their friends, learning new skills & trying new things.

About

The children & young people at Little Grove all have a learning disability and many of them have autism. This can make life more difficult for them and so for some; they may need more intensive support to help them overcome these challenges. For many, accessing mainstream services can be tricky & with few specialist services available, they have very limited social or leisure opportunities. For others, their behaviour or high levels of anxiety can prevent them from going out & doing regular activities such as swimming or visiting the shops. At Little Grove, we aim to cater to these children & young people by offering the personalised support they need to reach their potential. Every young person at Little Grove has their own success story to tell. They have grown in confidence, in independence and they now thoroughly enjoy lots of new opportunities that in the past, they may not have thought possible. FAQ

How do families pay for our services?

Our service is offered privately to families who usually use Direct Payments to cover the costs. We also work with families not yet in receipt of these, to create a package of care that suits them and which they can then request from their social worker.

How is the service funded?

We charge families for the service their young person receives. However this does not cover the full cost of running Little Grove so we also try to raise money through an annual Fundraising event. We receive no Government funding and rely on the generosity of lovely people to keep us ticking over.

Is there an age limit for the service?

We currently offer the service at Little Grove to anyone over the age of 8 but we do not have an upper age limit. We strive to offer a service that helps children & young people transition into adulthood so we aim to support them until such time as they no longer need us!

How can I support Little Grove?

We are always in need of items around the house or toys and equipment for the young people so please bear us in mind if you are having a clear out at home! Sponsorship

Little Grove is a social enterprise which means it operates as a limited company but with all the profit it makes, spent exclusively on the people that use the service. It is not registered as a charity because its long-term aim is to be self-sufficient and therefore, sustainable for the future. Little Grove has come a long way since we began in 2011 and has done so on parents contributions, a generous Landlord & a great deal of time, resources and financial backing from the Directors.
