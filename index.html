Little Grove

Little Grove Community Interest Company is a registered not-for-profit social enterprise based in Bushey, Hertfordshire. We work with children and young people who have a learning disability & provide them with support services during the day, in the evenings and during weekends & school holidays. We also offer regular overnight stays and are open all year round.
