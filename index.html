Little Grove

Little Grove Community Interest Company is a registered not-for-profit social enterprise based in Bushey, Hertfordshire. We work with children and young people who have learning disabilities & provide them with support services during the day, in the evenings and during weekends & school holidays. We also offer regular overnight stays and are open all year round. At Little Grove, we provide a comfortable, home-from-home environment in which children and young people can spend time with their friends, learning new skills & trying new things.


About

The children & young people at Little Grove all have a learning disability and many of them have autism. This can make life more difficult for them and so for some; they may need more intensive support to help them overcome these challenges. For many, accessing mainstream services can be tricky & with few specialist services available, they have very limited social or leisure opportunities. For others, their behaviour or high levels of anxiety can prevent them from going out & doing regular activities such as swimming or visiting the shops. At Little Grove, we aim to cater to these children & young people by offering the personalised support they need to reach their potential. Every young person at Little Grove has their own success story to tell. They have grown in confidence, in independence and they now thoroughly enjoy lots of new opportunities that in the past, they may not have thought possible. Sponsorship

Little Grove is a social enterprise which means it operates as a limited company but with all the profit it makes, spent exclusively on the people that use the service. It is not registered as a charity because its long-term aim is to be self-sufficient and therefore, sustainable for the future. Little Grove has come a long way since we began in 2011 and has done so on parents contributions, a generous Landlord & a great deal of time, resources and financial backing from the Directors. We are now actively seeking to fundraise some much needed money to help them sustain and develop the service so that many more families can benefit.
